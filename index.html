One decision - 13 seconds - One world that can end

You're dropped into two real historical nuclear close calls. Information arrives line by line. Press Space to continue. When the timer starts, choose fast, choose wiseley, choose correctly or see the end of the world.

How it works

  • Console feed: narration arrives line by line.
  • Press Space: advance when no choice is active.
  • Timed choices: some actions must be chosen before countdown ends.
  • Consequences: your choices change tension, trust, and outcomes.
